Blueberry Drop Cookies

When life brings you down, have these cookies to bring you up!

A delicious alternative to chocolate cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Recipe steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream shortening, sugar, egg, milk, almond extract and lemon zest. Mix well after the addition of each ingredient. Combine baking powder, flour and salt; blend into the mixture. Fold in blueberries. Cover and chill for 4 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to about 190 degrees celsius.
  3. Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets, about 1 and a half inches apart.
  4. Bake for about 15 minutes in the oven. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
