Enchilada Lasagna

A lasagna made perfectly to give joy to your life!

A lasagna for those who want to add more to the regular lasagna

Ingredients:

  • A tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • An onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pounds ground turkey
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with lime juice
  • 1 egg
  • A tablespoon of ground cumin
  • Cooking spray
  • 1 green onion, diced
  • 5 corn tortillas, halved
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 1 package small-curd cottage cheese

Recipe steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat, then add onion and garlic. Cook and stir until the onion is translucent for about 5 minutes. Stir in ground turkey; cook until it is no longer pink. Drain excess grease
  2. Stir enchilada sauce and diced tomatoes into the pot. Simmer until flavors combine, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Preheat the oven to about 190 degrees celsius.
  4. Mix cottage cheese, egg and cumin together in a small bowl.
  5. Spread 1/3 of the turkey sauce in the bottom of an 8-inch baking dish. Cover with 1/2 of the corn tortillas. Spread 1/2 of the cottage cheese mixture on top. Sprinkle 1/3 of the Mexican cheese on top. Repeat once more, ending with remaining sauce and cheese.
  6. Spray a large piece of aluminum foil with cooking spray; cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly. Remove aluminum foil and continue baking until the top is browned. Cool for 15 minutes before serving and garnish with green onion.
